About Mawsami cave

Mawsmai Cave is located in the East Khasi Hills district of Meghalaya, India ¹. It is a popular tourist attraction, known for its narrow passages, majestic caverns, and intricate formations ¹. The cave has a spacious opening, but it soon narrows into a tight path ². The interior is spacious, with some halls resembling an amphitheater ². Visitors can see constant dripping of water from the cave's roof and various forms of stalactites and stalagmites ².
