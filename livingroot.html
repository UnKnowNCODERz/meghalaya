About living root

Here are some facts about the Living Root Bridge ¹ ²:

  • - The Living Root Bridge is located in Meghalaya, India, and is considered a natural wonder.
  • - The bridge is made from the roots of the Ficus elastica tree.
  • - It takes 10 to 15 years for the roots to grow and form the bridge.
  • - The bridges can last up to 500 years.
  • - The double-decker root bridge in Cherrapunji and the single-decker root bridge in Shillong are two of the most well-known bridges.
  • - The bridges are recognized by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site.
